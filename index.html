Tapera Jungle Project

The Tapera Jungle project is a private initiative of Urupolpas, alongside many collaborators, such as the Agrofrut cooperative, in Urucara, and a group of botanical and nature enthusiasts. The project aims to recover ownership of lands, restore and protect them, while valuing this treasure of biodiversity, which has more than 100 species of trees.

The Tapera Jungle project takes place in the region of Urucará, Amazon, and combines Avoided Planned Deforestation (APD) and Avoided Unplanned Deforestation and Degradation (AUDD) activities. The primary objective of the project is to conserve 43,000 ha of Amazon rainforest through forest conservation and sustainable forest management.

Our NFTs

NFTs

Tapera Jungle Trees Collection is a collection of 8000 unique trees NFT's - unique digital collectibles living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT is linked to a real tree from the Tapera Jungle, with data of the tree: botanic name, diameter, height, characteristics, and GPS coordinate. Each tree is marked with a metallic plate with its identification number. Your Tapera Jungle NFT doubles you as a member of the Tapera Jungle Club, and grants to you a members-only special access and partnership for events, activities, and all information related to the project, as well as the chance to visit the forest and be an actor of this extraordinary Amazonian biodiversity conservation project. Visit www.taperajungle.com to know more.
